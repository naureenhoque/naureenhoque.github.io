Research

Research directions

My group develops secure, intelligent, and future-ready systems across four connected research themes.

01

AI for Cybersecurity

Machine learning methods for threat detection, cyber defense, vulnerability analysis, anomaly detection, and security operations.

  • Threat detection
  • Adversarial learning
  • Security analytics
02

Security of AI Systems

Trustworthy and resilient AI, including attacks on learning systems, privacy-preserving learning, explainability, and robust deployment.

  • Trustworthy AI
  • Privacy
  • Robustness
03

Secure Wireless Systems

Security and privacy for wireless, mobile, edge, Internet of Things, cyber-physical, and next-generation communication systems.

  • Wireless security
  • IoT and edge
  • NextG networks
04

Post-Quantum Security

Quantum-resistant cryptography and migration strategies for connected systems, constrained devices, and future network infrastructure.

  • Quantum-safe systems
  • Cryptographic agility
  • PQC deployment

Research vision

How can we build intelligent and connected systems that remain dependable as attackers, networks, and computing technologies evolve?
